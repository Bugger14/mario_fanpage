Super Mario

Mario is the main protoganist in the mario games, including but not limited to: super mario bros, super mario galaxy, and super mario odyssey.
he also appeared in other games where there isnt much of a story, these include the super smash series and the mario kart series.

Mario's main missions are almost always to save princess peach, which falls victim of kidnapping by bowser, a huge turtle dragon.

Physical appearance

Mario has a red hat with a red shirt, blue overalls, brown shoes, and a sexy moustache. His suit sometimes changes in different games
